Privacy Policy for Delete Facebook Messages Pro 2024

Effective Date: Monday, 26 August 2024

Introduction

Delete Facebook Messages Pro 2024 (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is a browser extension designed to help you manage, delete, and archive Facebook messages. This privacy policy outlines how we handle and protect your data when you use our extension.

Scope

This privacy policy applies exclusively to the use of the Delete Facebook Messages Pro 2024 browser extension. It does not cover any other applications, websites, or services provided by us or third parties.

Definitions

"Data": Refers to any information collected or used by the extension, such as names and profile images.

"Session": The period during which the extension is active on your browser, ending when the page is refreshed or closed.

Data Collection

Our extension collects the following data temporarily from Facebook:

  • Name: Names associated with messages
  • Image: Profile images related to these messages

We do not collect, store, or access the content of your messages, your Facebook login credentials, or any other personal information.

Data Usage

Temporary Storage: The collected data is stored only for the duration of the current session. Once the page is reloaded, the data is automatically deleted and is no longer accessible.

User Actions: Data is utilized to perform actions such as deleting or archiving messages.

Data Security

Access Control: Only you, the user, have access to the data through the extension. The data cannot be accessed by anyone else, including the developer of the extension.

Privacy Assurance: We do not store, share, or sell any data. Your data remains private and is exclusively used to provide the extension's functionality.

Data Deletion

Automatic Deletion: All collected data is deleted when the page is refreshed. No data persists beyond the current session.

Data Protection

Security Measures: We employ strong security measures to protect your data and ensure its privacy. The extension is designed to prioritize your data security, and we have no access to the data you manage with our tool.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services for data collection, storage, or processing. All data interaction is confined to your local browser environment.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices, operational needs, legal requirements, or regulatory needs. Any significant changes will be communicated through the extension’s update notes or via email if you have provided contact information. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
